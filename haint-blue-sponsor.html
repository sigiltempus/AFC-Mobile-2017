AFC Mobile Title Sponsor Announcement

Posted on Feb 1, 2017 in: Sponsorship

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

AFC MOBILE ANNOUNCES HAINT BLUE BREWING COMPANY AS TITLE SPONSOR FOR INAUGURAL SEASON



AFC Mobile is excited to announce Haint Blue Brewing Company as the club’s title sponsor.

“AFC Mobile will proudly represent the local community, so we really wanted our title sponsor to be tied to the area. We’re absolutely thrilled to partner with Haint Blue, who aligns perfectly with our pro-community values, as our first kit sponsor.” said AFC Mobile board member Jeff Garl. Haint Blue is Mobile’s first microbrewery since prohibition. The brewery will operate out of the old Crystal Ice warehouse on Monroe Street near Broad and Canal,with the Church Street Cemetery and the ghost of old Joe Cain right in its backyard. Haint Blue plans to open a taproom in the icehouse where Mobilians can gather to enjoy their beer and each other’s company
.
“We admire those who choose to create, and feel fortunate to partner with another organization whose ultimate goal is to add value to the quality of life in Mobile!” said Haint Blue founder Keith Sherrill.

AFC Mobile is looking forward to working alongside Haint Blue to continue to give Mobilians a soccer team, and a beer, they will be proud to call their own.

Like AFC Mobile and Haint Blue Brewing Co. on Facebook and follow @AFC_Mobile and @haintbluebrew on Twitter for further updates and announcements.

ABOUT AFC Mobile


AFC Mobile was formed in 2015 to strengthen the soccer community in Mobile by bringing a high level soccer team to the largest municipality along the Gulf Coast between St. Petersburg and New Orleans. AFC Mobile aims to provide local players an opportunity to continue to play the game at a competitive level, as well as to give the soccer community and City of Mobile a team to call their own.
